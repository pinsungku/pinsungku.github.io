About

I’m a Ph.D. student advised by Dr. Cindy Hsin-Liu Kao in Information Science at Cornell University. My research in Hybrid Body Lab focused on ubiquitous computing, and wearable on-skin technologies. My objective is to augment human body by enabling customizable sensing/actuator functions to modular wearable systems.
