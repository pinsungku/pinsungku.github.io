Patch-O

Pin-Sung Ku, Kunpeng Huang, Aditi Galada, Cindy Hsin-Liu Kao

Overview#

Patch-O interfaces are uniquely detachable and relocatable soft actuation units that can be sewn or attached to clothing or skin at various locations. We implemented three basic actuation primitives, including bending, expanding, and shrinking, using shape memory alloys (SMA).

Authors: Pin-Sung Ku, Kunpeng Huang, Aditi Galada, Cindy Hsin-Liu Kao
